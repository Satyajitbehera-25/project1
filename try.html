Village Highlights

Green Valley Lake

Our pristine lake offers excellent fishing, boating, and picnic spots with stunning mountain views. The perfect place for family outings and peaceful reflection.

Historic Square

Walk through our beautifully preserved historic square with buildings dating back to 1850. Explore unique shops, cafes, and our weekend farmers market.

Nature Trails

Over 20 miles of well-maintained hiking trails wind through our diverse landscape, from dense forests to open meadows filled with wildflowers.
